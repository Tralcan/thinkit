ThinkDrive Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

ThinkDrive respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application processes information and what data is collected when you use ThinkDrive.

Overview

ThinkDrive is designed with privacy as a core principle. The application does not require users to create accounts and does not collect personal information for marketing, advertising, analytics, or profiling purposes.

Voice Recordings and Transcriptions

When you record audio using ThinkDrive, speech-to-text transcription is performed entirely on your Apple device using Apple's built-in speech recognition technologies.

Audio recordings are not transmitted to ThinkDrive servers for transcription.

Once transcription has been completed on the device, the resulting text may be sent to trusted third-party Artificial Intelligence services solely for the purpose of generating summaries, extracting ideas, organizing information, and creating actionable tasks requested by the user.

Information We Collect

ThinkDrive does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share personal information about its users.

Specifically, ThinkDrive does not collect:

  • Name, address, phone number, or contact information.
  • Location data.
  • Advertising identifiers.
  • Behavioral analytics.
  • Marketing profiles.
  • Voice recordings on ThinkDrive servers.

Use of Artificial Intelligence Services

To provide its core functionality, ThinkDrive may send the transcription text generated on the device to trusted third-party Artificial Intelligence providers.

These providers process the text solely to generate summaries, extract tasks, organize ideas, and provide productivity-related insights requested by the user.

ThinkDrive does not use this information for advertising, tracking, user profiling, or marketing purposes.

Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple through the App Store.

ThinkDrive does not receive, process, or store payment card information.

Purchase and subscription information is handled directly by Apple according to Apple's privacy and payment policies.

Data Storage

User-generated content may be stored locally on the user's device and, if enabled by the user, synchronized through Apple services such as iCloud.

ThinkDrive does not maintain its own database of personal user information.

Third-Party Services

ThinkDrive may rely on third-party Artificial Intelligence providers to process transcription text and generate insights requested by the user.

These services are used exclusively to provide the functionality requested by the user and are not used for advertising or tracking purposes.

Children's Privacy

ThinkDrive is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page together with the updated revision date.
